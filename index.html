Welcome to Our Church Family

.

Our church is accredited by the New York District Church of the Nazarene and adheres to the Manual of the Church of the Nazarene, which outlines our articles of faith, government, and doctrine. We joyfully participate in the denomination's mission to "make Christlike disciples in the nations."

Our Mission Statement

"To be a Christ-centered community that loves God completely (Matthew 22:37), loves others unconditionally (Matthew 22:39), and makes disciples faithfully (Matthew 28:19-20)."
