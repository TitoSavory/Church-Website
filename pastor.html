Meet Our Pastor

Rev. Sase Dihal - Leading with faith, love, and compassion

Email Pastor

Pastor Sase Dihal

Pastor Sase has been faithfully serving at Queens Village Church of the Nazarene. With a pastor's heart and a teacher's mind, he brings dynamic biblical preaching and compassionate leadership to our congregation.

Education & Credentials

  • Master of Divinity, Nazarene Theological Seminary
  • Bachelor of Theology, Eastern Nazarene College
  • Ordained Elder in the Church of the Nazarene

With over two decades of ministry experience across the district, Pastor Sase has a proven track record of church growth and community transformation. His research focuses on urban ministry and community engagement.

Pastor Sase is married to Debbie, and together they have raised two wonderful children. When not engaged in ministry, he enjoys theological reading, cricket, and playing worship music.

A Message From Pastor David

"It's my joy and privilege to serve as your pastor here at Queens Village Church of the Nazarene. My prayer is that we would be a church that faithfully proclaims the Gospel, deeply loves one another, and actively serves our community."

I believe God has called us to be a light in our neighborhood, showing Christ's love through both our words and actions. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church home, we welcome you with open arms.

Join us as we seek to grow in Christ together, support one another in life's journey, and make a difference in our world for God's kingdom.

Rev. Sase Dihal
