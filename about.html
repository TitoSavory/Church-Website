Our History

Faithfully serving Queens Village

Our Story

1952 - Humble Beginnings

Founded by a small group of Nazarene families meeting in a rented storefront on Jamaica Avenue. Rev. Thomas Wilson served as the first pastor with a congregation of 23 members.

1957 - First Building

Purchased and renovated a small house on 222nd Street to serve as both sanctuary and parsonage. The congregation grew to 75 members under Pastor James Carter.

1965 - Expansion

Built the current sanctuary to accommodate growing attendance, which had reached 150. Started the church's first Sunday School program and youth ministry.

1980 - Community Outreach

Launched the Queens Village Food Pantry, which continues to serve hundreds of families each month. Added Wednesday night Bible study and prayer meetings.

1999 - Renovation

Completed major building renovations including updated sanctuary, new classrooms, and fellowship hall. Celebrated with a 300-person anniversary service.

2015-Present - New Vision

Under Pastor David Johnson's leadership, launched contemporary worship services, small group ministries, and expanded community service programs.

Our Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, our authoritative rule for faith and practice.

Salvation

We believe in salvation through faith in Jesus Christ, available to all who repent and believe.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and make disciples.

Holiness

We believe in entire sanctification as a work of God's grace subsequent to salvation.
